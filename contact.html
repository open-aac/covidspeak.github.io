Co-VidSpeak is not publicly accessible right now. We want to provide support to those who need it, but this is a free project and we can't affort to just open the floodgates. If you are in a situation where you can support implementing Co-VidSpeak to those who could use it, please reach out to us with your specific situation and how you can envision Co-VidSpeak helping.

To contact us, please fill out this Google Form and we will get back to you as quickly as we can.

Additionally, if you would like to support this project, please consider making a donation through ATMakers, or providing software development or speech therapy consulting support by joining us at openaac.org.
