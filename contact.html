Co-VidSpeak is available as a subscription service, but we also work with sponsoring organizations to fund additional opportunities. Right now we are funding free accounts for any medical groups needing access during the Covid-19 pandemic, with help from ATMakers. We are also actively looking for additional sponsors to expand the reach of our free initiative, so please contact us if you are interested!

If you need help funding Co-VidSpeak, or are interested in learning more about our work, you can contact us directly, just fill out this Google Form and we will get back to you as quickly as we can.

Additionally, if you would like to support this project, please consider making a donation through ATMakers, or providing software development or speech therapy consulting support by joining us at openaac.org. We are always looking for smart, dedicated people to help grow this project into the future.
