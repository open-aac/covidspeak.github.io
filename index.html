About Co-VidSpeak

Co-VidSpeak is a free open source, web-based video conferencing tool built for people who can't speak but still need to emotionally connection with those at a distance.

When someone is intubated, on a ventilator, or otherwise non-verbal -- especially when unwell -- they can greatly benefit from positive emotional connections with others. However, when quarantine or social distancing are in effect, physical proximity is not an option.

We built Co-VidSpeak as a free tool to help people in these sittuations because we've seen it ourselves. We have family member and friends who have been intubated, and can't imagine them having to be isolated from loved ones while enduring a major recovery.

How to Sign Up

Co-VidSpeak is currently available on a limited trial basis. We are working with hospitals and medical groups to implement Co-VidSpeak in medical settings to support individuals during the Covid-19 pandemic. You can see how it works using our .

If you are part of a group that could benefit from Co-VidSpeak, please reach out to us with details about your situation and how you think Co-VidSpeak could help.

We are offering this as a free service because we want to help. If you like what we are doing and want to help, you can donate through ATMakers to help fund our server and bandwidth costs. If you have software development of speech therapy experience and want to get involved, join us at openaac.org.
